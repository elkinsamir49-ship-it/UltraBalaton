    About the team race

    The Ultrabalaton Running Fest is an annual running event in Hungary that
    encircles Lake Balaton which is the largest freshwater lake in Europe.
    The course is unique and provides runners with stunning views of Lake
    Balaton and the surrounding region's cultural and historical landmarks.
    The race can be completed as a team or as an individual runner and has
    a 32-hour time limit. Runners traversing a distance of 221 kilometers,
    which is more than 5 marathon distances.
